Our Story

Born from a gap felt by students

"MedicineBank was built because students needed it — not because someone thought they should."

MedicineBank was informally established in October 2023 in response to a noticeable lack of reliable academic resource indexing for medical students. It began as a small initiative collecting, creating, and archiving summaries, MCQs, recorded lectures, and educational content in one unified platform.

Organic growth across Egypt and the Arab region led to hundreds of thousands of visits and strong community engagement — evolving MedicineBank into a structured, multi-sector initiative serving medicine, pharmacy, dentistry, and beyond.

03 — Academic Aid
🎓

Academic Aid

Structured tools, resources, and planning systems for students across all health sciences disciplines.

Academic Aid is the student-facing core of MedicineBank — providing MCQ banks, lecture summaries, study planners, group libraries, and public course catalogs. It serves medicine, pharmacy, and dentistry students with discipline-specific content and collaborative tools.
