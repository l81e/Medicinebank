Our Story

Born from a gap felt by students

MedicineBank was informally established in October 2023 in response to a noticeable lack of reliable academic resource indexing for medical students. It began as a small initiative collecting, creating, and archiving summaries, MCQs, recorded lectures, and educational content in one unified platform.

Organic growth across Egypt and the Arab region led to hundreds of thousands of visits and strong community engagement. This evolution turned MedicineBank into a structured, multi-sector initiative. In March 2026, MedicineBank received official accreditation from Mansoura National University.

Mission

Empower student personal development

To serve as the definitive resource hub for medical students — providing academic materials, research support, and professional opportunities that fuel individual and collective growth across institutions and borders.

Vision

A leading student-driven global foundation

To be the foremost student-led foundation fostering academic excellence, research innovation, and global connectivity — branching under the same name nationally and internationally.

Operational Arms

The Four Acting Sectors

LOG

Logistics

Manages internal operations, IT assets, academic aid archival, public event relations, and human resources to keep the initiative running seamlessly.

RES

Research

Advances research capacity through educational activities, project facilitation, mentorship, publication support, and management of MedicineBank's prospective research journal.

REL

Relations

Manages external affairs, institutional partnerships, collaborative networks, and long-term engagement to expand the initiative's academic and professional reach.

MED

Media

Oversees social media platforms, educational content campaigns, brand identity, and community outreach to grow the initiative's public presence.

Sector Details

What Each Sector Does

LOG

Logistics Sector

Responsible for the management and development of internal operations, IT assets, and means of academic aid archival. Handles public event-related logistics and human resources. The backbone that keeps every other sector running efficiently.

RES

Research Sector

Responsible for advancing research capacity within the initiative. Functions include organization of research methodology education, facilitation of research projects, mentorship opportunities, and support of publication efforts. RES also oversees the development of MedicineBank's prospective research journal, and uniquely operates with separate chiefs per college or school, coordinated by a chief coordinator.

REL

Relations Sector

Responsible for managing external affairs and institutional long-term engagement. Establishes and maintains partnerships, coordinates collaborative projects, and expands the initiative's professional and academic network — serving both RES and MED in their outward-facing activities.

Internal Structure

Inter-Sectoral Coordination

📋
Sector Secretaries Each sector designates a Secretary as the central coordination point, facilitating communication across sectors and reporting to their Chief or Vice Chief.
🔗
Cross-Sector Liaison Secretaries liaise with one another across all four sectors, ensuring timely information flow, alignment, and overall cohesion of the initiative.
📡
Each Sector's Own Protocol Every sector operates with its own inner coordination protocol, maintaining independence while staying aligned with the initiative's strategic direction.
