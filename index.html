Our Story

Born from a need
felt by students

MedicineBank was informally established in October 2023, born out of a noticeable absence of reliable, indexed academic resources for medical students. What began as a small collection of summaries, MCQs, recorded lectures, and educational content quickly became something far greater.

Organic growth across Egypt and the Arab region led to hundreds of thousands of visits and strong community engagement — evolving MedicineBank into a structured, multi-sector initiative. In March 2026, MedicineBank received official accreditation from Mansoura National University.

Operational Arms

The Four Acting Sectors

Each sector operates under a Chief and Vice Chiefs, ensuring efficiency, accountability, and continuous development in alignment with the initiative's strategic direction.

LOG

Logistics

Manages internal operations, IT assets, academic aid archival, public event relations, and human resources to keep the initiative running seamlessly.

RES

Research

Advances research capacity through educational activities, facilitation of projects, mentorship, publication support, and oversight of MedicineBank's prospective research journal.

REL

Relations

Manages external affairs, institutional partnerships, collaborative networks, and long-term engagement to expand the initiative's academic and professional reach.

MED

Media

Oversees social media platforms, educational content campaigns, brand identity, and community outreach to maintain and grow the initiative's public presence.

Internal Structure

Inter-Sectoral
Coordination

Each sector designates a Secretary who serves as the central point of coordination, facilitating communication and collaboration across sectors. Secretaries liaise with one another and report pertinent updates directly to their Chief or Vice Chief — ensuring timely information flow, alignment between sectors, and the overall cohesion of the initiative.
